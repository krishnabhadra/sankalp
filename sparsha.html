About the Conclave

RESEARCH.
INNOVATE.
CONNECT.

A signature research platform bringing together researchers, industry leaders, and participants for knowledge exchange and collaborative innovation.

SPARSHA 2027 is the flagship one-day hybrid research conclave at the core of SANKALP. It is designed as a high-visibility platform to connect participants with global research frontiers, industry innovation, and IEEE RAS leadership — while positioning the Kerala robotics ecosystem at a national and international level.

The conclave brings together researchers, industry leaders, and graduate participants to facilitate knowledge exchange, research dissemination, and collaborative innovation in neurorobotics, haptics, and intelligent systems.

It also serves as a public-facing component of the program, demonstrating the regional impact and outreach of the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society.

"A high-visibility, public-facing research platform demonstrating IEEE RAS regional impact — connecting researchers, industry, and the next generation of robotics innovators."
