PHASE I – School Students (Grades 8–12)
Foundational Program in Neurorobotics & Human–Machine Interaction
Duration: 3 Days
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
PRAGYAN
Pragyan Explorers is a structured foundational program designed to introduce school students to
the interdisciplinary domain of neurorobotics, human–machine interaction, and intelligent
systems. The program adopts an experiential learning approach, progressing from conceptual
understanding to hands-on implementation and finally to real-world exposure.

OVERALL PROGRAM OUTCOMES
At the end of the program, participants will:
● Understand the fundamentals of neurorobotics and human–machine interaction
● Gain hands-on experience in electronics and robotic system building
● Develop skills in sensing, control, and basic autonomy
● Connect technical learning with real-world healthcare applications
● Build curiosity, creativity, and motivation toward STEM and innovation
