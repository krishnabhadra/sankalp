About SANKALP

A RESEARCH PLATFORM.

SANKALP bridges the gap between academic theory and industry-grade research, equipping participants with real skills in AI, Robotics, and Neural Interfaces.

SANKALP 2026 is IEEE RAS Kerala Chapter's flagship Technical Education Program — a 32-day multi-institutional initiative that goes far beyond a conventional workshop or hackathon.

The program addresses a critical gap: engineering graduates with strong theoretical foundations but limited exposure to cutting-edge research areas like Brain-Computer Interfaces, Haptics, and Cyber-Physical Systems shaping Industry 5.0.

With mentorship from premier academic institutions and leading industry partners, SANKALP offers a uniquely structured progression from foundational learning to prototype development and industry simulation.

"it's a research program platform combining education, innovation, and industry. Smart Advanced Neurorobotics & Knowledge-driven Automation & Learning Program."

Research Tracks

CHOOSE YOUR
SPECIALIZATION

Three deep-dive tracks tailored to cutting-edge research domains. Each track delivers hands-on prototyping and a real working system by program end.

01
🧠
Multimodal Neurorobotics
EMG · EEG · Gesture · Vision

Build systems that decode human neural signals and motion to control robotic actuators — from EMG-driven prosthetics to vision-gesture hybrid interfaces.

  • Surface EMG signal acquisition & processing
  • EEG-based intent decoding
  • Gesture + computer vision fusion
  • Real-time motor control systems
  • BCI architecture design
Output → EMG/EEG-controlled robotic arm prototype
02
🤚
Haptics & Tactile Interaction
Force Feedback · Touch Sensing · HRI

Engineer the sense of touch in robotics — designing tactile sensors, force-feedback controllers, and human-robot interaction systems for teleoperation and assistive tech.

  • Tactile sensor design and fabrication
  • Force feedback algorithms
  • Human-robot touch interaction models
  • Teleoperation with haptic rendering
  • Soft robotics & compliant mechanisms
Output → Haptic feedback glove with tactile rendering system
03
⚙️
Cyber-Physical Systems
IoT · Embedded AI · Edge Computing

Design intelligent systems where computation and physical processes are tightly coupled — from smart manufacturing cells to AI-at-the-edge industrial automation.

  • IoT sensor networks & protocols
  • Embedded AI with TensorFlow Lite
  • Digital twin modeling
  • Real-time OS & deterministic control
  • Industrial automation integration
Output → AI-enabled CPS node with digital twin integration
Graduate Bootcamp

THE ELITE
RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

Designed exclusively for graduate students serious about research — an intensive mentorship-driven environment to develop original work.

RESEARCH.
IDEATE.
BUILD.
PUBLISH.

The Graduate Research Bootcamp is the crown jewel of SANKALP — an intensive, mentored experience where select participants develop original research ideas, read foundational papers, and build systems worthy of IEEE publication.

Participants work directly with faculty from premier institutions and are guided by the Graduate Student Advisory Committee (GSAC), ensuring both technical rigor and research methodology are firmly instilled.

Every bootcamp participant leaves with a structured research proposal, a working prototype, and a clear pathway toward IEEE RA-L or ICRA submission.

📄

Research Paper Reading

Structured sessions on landmark papers in Neurorobotics, HRI, and CPS with guided critique.

💡

Proposal Development

Step-by-step guidance in formulating research problems, hypotheses, and experimental designs.

🧑‍🏫

GSAC Mentorship

One-on-one and group mentorship by GSAC members and priemier institutions faculty mentors.

🏛️

Priemier academic institutions involvement

Lab visits, joint sessions, and collaborative research frameworks various Robotics group.

📊

IEEE Publication Pathway

Guided toward submitting to IEEE RA-L, ICRA, or IROS with structured writing workshops.
